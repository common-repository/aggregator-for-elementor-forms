== Description ==

Tired of not remembering where all your Elementor forms are at? The Elementor Form Aggregator Plugin is a powerful tool that simplifies managing your Elementor forms. No more hassle of searching for forms scattered across your website. This plugin brings all your Elementor forms together in one convenient admin page, allowing you to easily edit them and customize notification emails.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How does the plugin simplify form management? =

This plugin eliminates the need to search for scattered forms across your website. By bringing all Elementor forms together in one central admin page, it provides a convenient and efficient way to edit, customize, and manage your forms.

= Does the plugin affect the performance of my website? =

No, the Elementor Form Aggregator Plugin is optimized for performance and is designed to have no impact on your website's speed and efficiency.
